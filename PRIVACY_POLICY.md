# Privacy Policy for Spotted

**Last Updated:** November 10, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Spotted ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the Spotted mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, age, email address, phone number
- **Profile Information**: Photos, bio, interests, occupation, education, height, hometown
- **Profile Prompts**: Responses to conversation starters and personality questions
- **User Content**: Messages, voice memos, stories, check-in captions
- **Verification Information**: Selfie photos for identity verification
- **Preferences**: Looking for (friends/dating/networking), activity preferences, filters

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically
- **Location Data**: Real-time location when you check in at venues
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent, interactions with other users
- **Analytics Data**: App performance, crashes, errors

### 1.3 Information From Other Sources
- **Social Media**: If you sign in with Apple or other providers, we receive basic profile information
- **User Reports**: Information provided when users report or block others

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 To Provide Our Services
- Create and manage your account
- Show you to other users in your area
- Enable check-ins at locations
- Facilitate matches and connections
- Deliver messages and notifications
- Display stories to your connections

### 2.2 To Improve Our Services
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Develop new features
- Fix bugs and improve performance
- Conduct research and development

### 2.3 For Safety and Security
- Verify user identity
- Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations

### 2.4 For Communication
- Send push notifications about matches, messages, and nearby check-ins
- Provide customer support
- Send important updates about our service

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 With Other Users
- **Public Profile**: Name, age, photos, bio, interests visible to other users
- **Location**: Your check-in location visible to nearby users
- **Stories**: Visible to users who have matched with you
- **Activity Status**: Online status, last active timestamp

### 3.2 With Service Providers
We share data with third-party service providers who help us operate Spotted:
- Cloud hosting (Firebase/Google Cloud)
- Analytics (Firebase Analytics)
- Crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)
- Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

### 3.3 For Legal Reasons
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Law enforcement requests
- Protection of rights, property, or safety

### 3.4 Business Transfers
If Spotted is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

## 4. Your Rights and Choices

### 4.1 Access and Control
- **View Data**: Access your personal data through the app settings
- **Edit Profile**: Update your profile information anytime
- **Delete Content**: Delete messages, photos, and stories
- **Export Data**: Request a copy of your data

### 4.2 Privacy Settings
- **Location Sharing**: Control when location is shared (only during check-ins)
- **Profile Visibility**: Control who can see your profile
- **Blocking**: Block users from contacting or viewing you
- **Activity Status**: Hide online status and last active time

### 4.3 Marketing Communications
- Opt out of promotional emails
- Disable push notifications in device settings
- Manage notification preferences in app

### 4.4 Account Deletion
You can delete your account at any time through Settings. This will:
- Remove your profile from the app
- Delete your photos and stories
- Remove your matches and conversations
- Erase your check-in history

**Note**: Some information may be retained for legal or operational purposes (e.g., to prevent banned users from creating new accounts).

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Most data deleted within 30 days
- **Legal Retention**: Some data retained longer for legal compliance
- **Backup Systems**: Data in backups deleted within 90 days

## 6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Monitoring for suspicious activity

However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.

## 7. Location Data

### 7.1 How We Use Location
- Display nearby users when checking in
- Show you on the map at your check-in location
- Suggest popular venues near you
- Provide location-based matching

### 7.2 Location Sharing Control
- Location only shared when you actively check in
- You can check out anytime to stop sharing
- Location not tracked in the background
- Precise location stored only for check-ins

## 8. Children's Privacy

Spotted is **NOT** for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover a user is under 18, we will delete their account immediately.

## 10. Your Regional Privacy Rights

### 10.1 European Union (GDPR)
If you're in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR:
- **Right to Access**: Obtain a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object**: Object to certain processing activities
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

**Legal Basis**: We process your data based on:
- Contract performance (providing our services)
- Legitimate interests (improving and securing our service)
- Your consent (for optional features)
- Legal obligations (complying with laws)

### 10.2 California (CCPA/CPRA)
If you're a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:
- **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect and how we use it
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

**California residents**: We do not "sell" personal information as defined by CCPA.

### 10.3 Other Regions
We comply with privacy laws in all jurisdictions where we operate.
